Multi-Agent AI Architecture

Three specialized agents — Planner, Scorer, and Summarizer — collaborate in real-time to find, evaluate, and explain the optimal green route for your journey.

Real-Time Carbon Scoring

Every route gets a live CO₂ footprint score, comparing transport modes and showing you the exact grams of carbon you'll save.

Air Quality Routing

Avoid pollution hotspots with real-time AQI data. Routes dynamically adjust to keep you breathing clean air.

Scenic Discovery Mode

AI identifies beautiful detours — parks, landmarks, waterfronts — adding joy to your commute with minimal time overhead.
